# Durian Use Case

Use this reference to calibrate `three-angle-sales-pitch`. It replaces the carrot example with durian so future agents do not overfit to the original source video.

## Input

- Product: fresh Musang King durian
- Audience: first-time or hesitant buyers in a city
- Channel: short video, livestream, or sales chat
- Constraints: do not invent origin certification, celebrity endorsements, health effects, or exact sweetness scores unless supplied by the seller

## Expected Three Angles

### Proof Angle

Buyer job: "I want confidence this expensive fruit is worth trying."

Script:
"This is for people who want to try real durian without guessing at a random stall. We open it at the ripe window, show the flesh texture on camera, and pack only the creamy sections, not the watery ones. If you care about the first bite, judge by color, aroma, and texture before you judge by price."

Why it works: it gives visible selection criteria and reduces quality uncertainty.

Evidence needed: actual variety, origin, packing time, replacement policy, and footage of the opened durian.

### Friction Angle

Buyer job: "I am curious, but I worry about the smell, mess, and wasting money."

Script:
"Durian is not a casual snack for everyone, and that is exactly why you should start small. Do not buy a whole shell on your first try. Take one packed portion, chill it, eat it after dinner, and decide whether the creamy, almond-like finish is your thing."

Why it works: it admits the objection instead of pretending durian is universally easy.

Evidence needed: portion size, storage guidance, delivery packaging, and return/replacement rules.

### Scene Angle

Buyer job: "I want a memorable moment, not just a fruit order."

Script:
"Bring this to a late-night gathering and it becomes the table's main event. Some people will refuse the first bite, someone will become the durian expert, and everyone will remember who brought it. You are not just buying fruit; you are buying the story people retell tomorrow."

Why it works: it reframes durian as a social scene and status trigger.

Evidence needed: occasion fit, serving size, safe transport, and honest aroma warning.

## Correctness Checks

- The three scripts sell the same durian through different buyer jobs.
- The scripts do not rely on a fake celebrity, fake origin, or fake quality metric.
- Each script includes a different kind of proof: visible quality, reduced risk, or remembered occasion.
- The set can be tested with three short-video variants using the same product footage.
