# Honest Friction

```yaml
style_id: honest-friction
display_name: Honest Friction
inspired_by: source-agnostic objection-handling sales patterns
source_safety: Uses transparent objection handling, not any protected wording, celebrity identity, or endorsement.
best_for: Products with obvious buyer resistance, learning curve, smell, mess, price anxiety, setup cost, or social risk.
avoid_when: The seller wants to hide a real drawback or cannot offer a practical mitigation.
formula: name the resistance -> validate it -> reduce the first step -> show who should not buy -> fair close
evidence_needed: Portion size, storage guidance, delivery packaging, first-time buyer advice, refund or replacement rules.
durian_example: "Durian is not for everyone, and that is the point. If you are worried about smell or wasting money, do not start with a whole shell. Take one chilled portion, eat it after dinner, and decide whether the creamy finish is your thing. If you need a clean, odorless office snack, skip this. If you want to understand why people chase this fruit, start small."
verification_test: Test whether hesitant buyers comment with fewer smell, mess, or waste objections after the pitch.
```
